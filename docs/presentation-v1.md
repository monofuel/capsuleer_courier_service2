### Presentation notes

original v1 CCS presentation
short and sweet!

Capsuleer Courier Service: Together, we can bridge the Galaxy.

Requesting items through the service is easy!
Visit one of our post boxes, enter the item type and quantity desired, and then bravely submit your request. The transaction will cost GAS, but otherwise there is no fee. Our generosity knows bounds.

Our service will show a thrilling display of deliveries. These deliveries are each waiting for a hero! Or anyone really, we're not picky. as long as they can provide an item and click the deliver button it's probably fine.

To serve your fellow Capsuleer and fulfill a delivery, you can place their desired item in the Smart Storage Unit and then click Deliver. You will be immediately given Likes. These Likes will confirm the transaction and perpetuate the illusion of productivity.

Once a fellow Capsuleer has transported your goods across the stars to our Super intelligent storage unit and hit deliver, your item will show up as ready for Pickup for you. Here you can marvel at the name of the charitable soul who probably didn't have anything better to do. Clicking Pick Up will move the item to your ephemeral inventory for you to scoop into your ship's cargo hold.

Successfully delivering items for other Capsuleers will earn likes for your account.
- You cannot spend likes.
- You cannot trade likes.
- There are no achievements and no milestones.
- These Likes do not show up in your wallet.
- They do not show up on your ship.
- these Likes will not help fill the empty void of space.
- We also can't promise that they will make you feel any less lonely.

Each act of generosity is eternally etched into the Blockchain. Your generosity for your fellow Capsuleers will be remembered forever.
